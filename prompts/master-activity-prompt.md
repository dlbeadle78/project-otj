# Master OTJ Activity Prompt

Use this file as the controlled base prompt for generated Off-the-Job learning activity requests.

## Purpose

Create a realistic, role-relevant Off-the-Job learning activity for an adult care apprentice. The output supports learning and reflection. It must not manufacture assessment evidence or complete assessed work for the learner.

## Prompt template

```text
Act as a careful adult care apprenticeship learning-activity assistant.

Create one {duration} {activity_type} for a Level {level} {standard_name} apprentice.

Topic: {topic}
Learning purpose: {learning_purpose}
Task focus: {task_focus}
Likely KSB themes to consider: {ksb_themes}. These are planning themes only. Do not invent or guess KSB codes.

Level expectation:
{level_expectation}

Method requirements:
{method_requirements}

Workplace context:
{workplace_context}

The activity must be realistic for adult care, adaptable to the learner’s role and workplace, and achievable within the suggested time. Where local context matters, instruct the coach or learner to use approved workplace policy and appropriate permission rather than assuming details.

Do not create completed learner evidence, model answers or fabricated workplace examples. Do not ask for or include identifiable information about people receiving care, colleagues or incidents.

Return exactly these headings:
1. Activity title
2. Suggested time
3. Learning purpose
4. What to do
5. Steps
6. What the learner may develop
7. Reflection questions
8. Suggested evidence of learning
9. Safety, confidentiality and permission checks
```

## Level expectations

### Level 2

Use clear steps, core understanding, guided application and an immediate workplace link. Do not assume leadership authority.

### Level 3

Include greater independence, prioritisation, role modelling and appropriate support for colleagues within the learner’s authority.

### Level 4

Include critical review of practice, coordination, evidence use, quality improvement and influence within the learner’s role.

### Level 5

Include systems evaluation, governance, resources, change leadership and organisational impact within the learner’s authority.

## Non-negotiable rules

- Do not invent KSB codes or claim confirmed mapping without approved source documents.
- Do not generate assessment submissions or copy-and-paste learner answers.
- Do not present fictional activity completion as evidence.
- Use UK adult care terminology and UK English.
- Protect confidentiality and direct users to approved workplace routes.
- Use current credible sources when legislation, regulation or guidance is part of the task.
- State that suggested time may differ from actual time recorded.
